    About Timothy Reyes

    • About Timothy Reyes

      I had visited my grandmothers house in Chicago some time ago. While there she had enlightened me with stories of trials and tribulations of my grandfathers occupation as a computer technician back in the 1970's or so. My grandfather had always been a tech savvy guy, but I never knew where it all began. I now know why I am so eager to learn the latest that technology has to offer. For the past 10 or so years I have worked at my father-in-laws company, Compix Media, Inc. In a nutshell, Compix Media creates broadcast graphic systems for video overlays, like when watching the News or Sports channel. Our main market sectors are education, sports, government, and broadcast. There, I have worked in technical support, sales, IT, marketing, and graphic/web design. My main focuses have been towards technical support. Although I was self taught at all levels, I built a pretty solid foundation of who I was, and what I want to become.
